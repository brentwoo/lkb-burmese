# Tense
#=========

# 2014-01-23 BPW - (a:124) "time reference is recovered from the context of situation". There are no explicit tense markers. The meanings of the various sentence-final particles, which are considered to encode "realis" "irrealis" "punctative/change of state", etc., coincide somewhat with our notion of tense.
# te = realis (RLS); non-future; habitual; past; generic events
# me = irrealis (IRR); future;

# Negation #
############

# 2014-01-22 BPW - negation is achieved with a negative particle 'ma' prefixed to the verb. Sentences with negative polarity REQUIRE a negative verb-clause marker, one of the following. Independent clause: hpu (informative), ne (imperative). Dependent clause: hpe ("without"), hkin ("before"), mahcin ("between")
# 2014-01-23 BPW - hpu is omitted in subordinate clauses. ma is omitted in "extremely informal speech" (a:144)
